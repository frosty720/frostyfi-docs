# FAQ

## General

**Q: What is FrostyFi?**
A: A Web3-native platform for building AI-powered workflows with blockchain automation.

**Q: Do I need crypto to use it?**
A: Yes, subscription requires USDC on Base chain. Free tier available for testing.

**Q: Where is it hosted?**
A: flow.frostylabs.ai (frontend), Oracle Cloud VPS (backend)

## Technical

**Q: What models are supported?**
A: All major providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, xAI, Groq, and more) are available through a single gateway — every chat model is routed through OpenRouter. See [Models & Usage](/docs/models).

**Q: Can I use my own API keys?**
A: No — LLM access is managed by FrostyFi through OpenRouter, with a per-user monthly budget (like Cursor). You don't bring or manage provider keys.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes — user-scoped isolation, and blockchain signing goes through ThirdWeb Vault so private keys never touch your workflow config.

**Q: Can I self-host?**
A: Not yet — FrostyFi is SaaS-only today. A self-host option (Docker/Coolify) is on the [roadmap](/docs/roadmap).

## Billing

**Q: How do subscriptions work?**
A: Subscriptions are paid in USDC on Base through the `FrostySubscription` smart contract — your tier is read directly from the chain against your wallet. No credit card, no NFT. See [Subscriptions & Pricing](/docs/pricing).

**Q: Can I cancel?**
A: Yes — simply don't renew. Your tier lapses on-chain when the subscription expires.

**Q: Refunds?**
A: Contact hello@frostylabs.ai
