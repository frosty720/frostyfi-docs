# Models & Usage

FrostyFi routes **all chat models through [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai)** — a
single gateway in front of every major provider. This page explains the model picker, how
monthly usage scales with the model you choose, and the **≈ calls left** meter.

## One gateway, one picker

![The model picker, with Text / Image / Video tabs and the calls-left estimate](/screenshots/model-picker.png)

Instead of separate provider integrations and per-provider keys, FrostyFi sends every chat
request through OpenRouter. That means:

- **One curated picker.** Add or remove a model in one place; no parallel
  tool/vision tables to keep in sync.
- **One spend control.** Each user's usage runs through a per-user OpenRouter key with a
  hard monthly cap (more below).
- **App attribution.** Every inference call carries FrostyFi's attribution headers, so the
  app appears on OpenRouter's public rankings and analytics.

Models come in two pools:

- **Free models** — the always-on `:free` set (strong open models at $0, e.g. Gemma,
  DeepSeek, Qwen, GPT-OSS). New `:free` models on OpenRouter are surfaced automatically.
- **Paid models** — a curated flagship set.

**Model access is binary — `free` or `paid`.** The free tier sees only free models; **both
Pro and Enterprise see every paid model.** There is no "this model is Enterprise-only."

## Monthly usage scales with the model

How much you can do each month depends on your [subscription tier](/docs/pricing) **and on
the models you pick** — that's the fair part. A call to a lightweight model and a call to a
frontier model are very different amounts of compute, so your allowance is measured by
usage rather than a flat call count. Cheaper models stretch much further; premium models
use more of your allowance per call.

| Tier | Monthly usage |
|------|---------------|
| **Free** | A flat **50 calls/month** on **free models only**. |
| **Pro** | A generous monthly allowance — roughly **400–4,700 premium calls/month**, depending on the models you use. |
| **Enterprise** | A larger allowance — roughly **1,500–18,000 premium calls/month**, depending on the models you use. |

When you reach your monthly allowance, FrostyFi shows a graceful "limit reached" message
and it resets at the start of each month. Free and fast models remain available to Pro and
Enterprise without drawing down the premium allowance.

## The "calls left" estimate

The usage meter translates your remaining monthly allowance into an **≈ N calls left**
estimate **for the model you currently have selected** — never a raw cost figure:

- Switch to a **cheaper** model and the estimate goes **up**.
- Switch to a **pricier** model and it goes **down**.

It's the same allowance either way — the meter just gives you an honest, intuitive sense of
what's left on the model you're using. The estimate uses a representative tokens-per-call
profile, so it's a guide, not a contract.

The **free tier** meter is simpler: it just shows calls used against the flat **50/month**
allowance.

## Cost tracking

FrostyFi records the real per-call cost reported by OpenRouter into a single usage ledger
per user. That ledger powers the meter, enforces budgets, and is the same accounting layer
that will support PERMA token usage in the future.
